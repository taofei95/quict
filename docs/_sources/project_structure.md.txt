## QuICT.core

Quantum circuit model including qbit, serveral kinds of 
quantum gates and quantum circuits.

## QuICT.algorithm

A variety of quantum algorithms.

## QuICT.qcda

The glossary **qcda** is the abbreviation of **Quantum Circuit
Design Automation**. This submodule takes a number of advanced
algorithms to achieve quantum circuit synthesis, mapping
and optimization.

## QuICT.tools

A collections of tools.

## QuICT.lib

Library which transform simple QuICT circuit representation into
some other forms.
